About Matt Krueger

I've built Sales Funnels, Retailer Websites, Web App Menus for the Restaurant Industry, and many others like the aforementioned.

At first, I tried to build websites using Web-Builders like Wordpress or Wix, but they limited me in all sorts of important ways. If websites were food, I wasn't making a home-cooked meal: I was just buying it packed frozen from the nearest convenience store. Since then, I've turned away from web builders and started making them from scratch using HTML and CSS.

At last, I finally had the power at my fingertips. Before, I couldn't be realistic about offering anything in terms of style. I couldn't make my my own animations, edits, minigames, ...nothing! But now, I can build something that I will guarantee no one else has on their websites, and use that experience to benefit others by carrying the holographic torch in their favor.

I can also do Graphical Art, Photography, Film, Music, and I have a specific passion to Game Design and Programming.
